Welcome to pHresh

Our pHresh water bottle uses natural minerals to provide you with alkaline water on the go. The replaceable pH filters ensure that your water is ready to drink in under 10 minutes.

Our Product

Our BPA-free bottle is made from recyclable plastic and comes equipped with a replaceable pH filter system. The filters contain natural minerals like Activated Carbon, Maifan stones, Tourmaline beads, Zeolite, and essential Mineral stones (Calcium, Magnesium, and Potassium).

Each filter lasts for approximately 90 days and can be easily replaced.
